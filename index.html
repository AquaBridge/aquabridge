About FastBuilder

FastBuilder is a simple gamemode. You bridge across a certain distance in the shortest amount of time. At AquaBridge, we try to reinvent the wheel for absolutely no effective reason.

The FastBuilder gamemode was originally invented on McPlayHD.net by McPlayHD and TROOP3R.
