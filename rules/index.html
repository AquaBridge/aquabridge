Guidelines

Server Rules

Rule 1

1. No Cheating

Self-explanatory. We don't want to see you using any form of cheating to gain an unfair advantage.

Rule 2

Keep it light.

Don't spam chat (or with commands), no harsh profanity, no racism/sexism/discrimination of any kind, no harassing players.

Rule 3

Lying to staff members.

Lying to staff members will have its own consequences. Be mature and don't lie. We will find out.

Rule 4

Respect staff decisions

Use this for punishments, appeal direction, impersonation, false reports, or ban evasion if that applies to your server.

Rule 5

Advertising

Advertising is not allowed other than inside of #self-promotion on our Discord.

Rule 6

Respect staff decisions

Any decision any staff makes is final and non-negotiable. You can however create a support ticket on our Discord to possibly lower your punishment duration. Staff may punish you without giving a reason or evidence regardless of the situation.
